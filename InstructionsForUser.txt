
In the current patch, LoL-Bot only works on [1920x1080] monitor resolution, and [1280x720] League of Legends Client resolution.



Note:	Higher resolution monitors can scale down to [1920x1080] in "Display settings", accessed by right clicking on the desktop.

	LoL-Bot will work properly after changing to [1920x1080] monitor resolution and [1280x720] Client resolution.



Two methods for changing to the correct in-game League of Legends settings are listed below.

Complete method 1 -OR- method 2 for LoL-Bot to work properly while in-game.


___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Method 1: Manually change settings while in a custom game.
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________


(Required)

1. Hit [Esc] to open the settings while in game.

2. Reset all in-game settings to default. (left click "Restore Defaults" at the top right on each settings tab.)

___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________


(Required)

1. Select the "GAME" tab.

2. Disable "Attack move on cursor".

___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________


(Optional)

1. Select the "HOTKEYS" tab

2. Enable "Quick Cast All"

3. Disable "Replace Quick Cast with Quick Cast With Indicator in the quickbind UI"

___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________



Or, complete Method 2


___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Method 2: Replacing PersistedSettings.json
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________


Login to the League of Legends Client (BUT DO NOT ENTER A GAME)

Replace PersistedSettings.json in "C:\Riot Games\League of Legends\Config" with the PersistedSettings.json in LoL-Bot/data
